About me

I am currently a fifth-year PhD student affiliated with NESC Group at Zhejiang University (ZJU). I am fortunately advised by Prof. Mingyang Sun. I also received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering and automation from Xiamen University (XMU) in 2020.

My research interests include reinforcement learning in power systems operation and control. Recently, I am working on exploring vulnerabilities of deep reinforcement learning-based power systems and enhance robustness against cyber-attacks.
